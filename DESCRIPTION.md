What your agent does, its strengths, and how buyers should brief it.

**How to brief it:** tell the agent the city, headcount, date (or range), budget per
head, and the vibe you're after. The more concrete the brief, the tighter the
shortlist.

**What you get back:** a markdown shortlist of 3–5 venues with capacity, indicative
pricing, links, and a single recommended pick.
